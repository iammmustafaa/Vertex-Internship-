Day 1 Notes (Canva Fundamentals)
What is Canva?
Canva is an online design platform that allows users to create visual content easily without needing advanced graphic design skills. It provides ready-made templates, fonts, images, and design elements that help in making professional-looking designs such as social media posts, presentations, posters, and more. It is widely used by students, marketers, and businesses for quick and effective design work.
Importance of Design in Branding:
Design plays a very important role in building a brand’s identity. A well-designed visual creates a strong first impression and helps people recognize a brand easily. Good design also makes content more attractive and understandable, which increases audience engagement. Consistent use of colors, fonts, and style helps in creating trust and professionalism. In today’s digital world, strong visual design is essential for standing out and communicating messages effectively.
